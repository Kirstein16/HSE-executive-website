Services

Understanding the needs and requirement of each project, client and country is foundamental to the success of the project. We bridge that gaps in terms of HSE mindset/culture, client versus main contractor and subcontractors management systems and standards. We review client and project HSE requirements, and guides the project team in terms of required preparations. Addtionally, we prepare the HSE documentations and procedures to support site execution.

Our team of HSE specialists are capable to provides supports during execution in the following but not limited items:

  • 1. Excavation and below zero works
  • 2. Lifting operations
  • 3. Hot works
  • 4. Work at height
  • 5. Work in confined spaces
  • 6. Electrical works
  • 7. Non Destructive Tests
  • 8. Pipe cleanings (Pickling and flushing)
  • 9. Pre-commissioning of systems, units, equipment and the plant
  • 10. Preparation of task specific risk analysis and permit to work
  • 11. Preparation of task specific HSE training materials and facilitate the trainings
  • 12. Preparation and performing of task specific toolbox talks
  • 13. Supervision of work activities and provision of HSE guidance
  • 14. Report non conformities, incidents and investigate the root causes
  • 15. Analysis incident reports, derive corrective measures and follow up implementation
  • 16. Gather data for client KPIs and submit report

    • Additionally, we prepare and provide in-house and site task specific HSE trainings to leaders, management and business deccision makers to improve HSE culture, awareness and preparedness towards successful and safe business.
